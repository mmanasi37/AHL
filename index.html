About AHL

Home-Grown.
Papua New Guinean.

Andro Holdings Limited (AHL) is a proudly locally owned company founded in Wewak, East Sepik Province. For over a decade, AHL has been a key driver of infrastructure development across the province — delivering government and DDA-funded civil works and professional security services that directly benefit communities.

Operating across two core divisions — Civil Engineering and Security Services — AHL employs hundreds of Papua New Guineans, champions local education, and builds lasting foundations for a stronger East Sepik Province.
